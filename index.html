About

I am a graduate of the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh (B.Sc. Engineering, 2019–2025; CGPA: 2.99/4.00). My academic training integrates computational materials science and design engineering, with emphasis on Density Functional Theory (DFT), sustainable and biodegradable composites, and computer-aided design (CAD) for product development.

Research Interests

My research spans computational modeling, sustainable materials design, and advanced manufacturing. I investigate how atomic-scale simulation and data-driven modeling can accelerate the discovery and optimization of next-generation materials and devices.

Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Electronic Structure

Simulation of perovskite and hybrid materials for optoelectronic and energy applications.

Biodegradable and Sustainable Composites

Use of natural fillers and polymer matrices to achieve mechanical performance with reduced environmental impact.

Computational Materials Design

Data-driven property prediction and multi-objective optimization using machine learning.

Three-Dimensional CAD and Product Design

Modeling, visualization, and design-for-manufacture using SolidWorks, AutoCAD, and KeyShot.

Advanced Characterization and Simulation Coupling

Linking first-principles results with experimental measurements to validate structure–property relationships.

Nanomaterials and Smart Polymers

Investigation of nanostructured and stimuli-responsive systems for biomedical and flexible devices.

Selected Projects

DFT Study of ASiF3 Perovskites

First-author work using density functional theory (Materials Studio) to study how hydrostatic pressure tunes the band gap and optical behavior of ASiF3 (A = Na, K, Rb) for potential optoelectronic applications.

Eggshell Filler Hybrid Composites (ICCHE 2023)

Conference project evaluating eggshell-based natural fillers on the mechanical properties and biodegradability of hybrid polymer composites, presented at the International Conference on Chemical, Materials & Energy Engineering (ICCHE 2023).

Nickel–Metal Hydride (NiMH) Batteries Study

Literature-based study of nickel–metal hydride (NiMH) batteries, focusing on working principles, material choices, and performance characteristics for energy-storage applications.

Material Selection for Heat Exchangers

Poster work using CES EduPack to compare candidate materials for heat exchangers based on thermal efficiency, corrosion resistance, and cost optimization.

3D CAD and Product Design

Design of complex parts and assemblies using SolidWorks and AutoCAD, with visualization in KeyShot and emphasis on design-for-manufacture.
